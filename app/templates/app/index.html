What is ToTimer?

ToTimer is a pomodoro timer which focuses on helping you complete your tasks with efficiency. Works both on desktop and mobile. This timer is inspired by the Pomodoro Technique developed by Francesco Cirillo.

The Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is created by Francesco Cirillo for a more productive way to work and study. The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. Each interval is known as a pomodoro, from the Italian word for 'tomato', after the tomato-shaped kitchen timer that Cirillo used as a university student. - Wikipedia

Using ToTimer

  1. Add tasks
  2. Start the Focus timer
  3. Work until you hear the alarm
  4. Take a Short 5 minutes break
  5. Repeat 2-4 for four times in total
  6. Take a Long 15 minutes break
  7. Repeat from second step
